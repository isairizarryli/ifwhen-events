About If/When

The heart behind the hosting.

If/When Events was created for people who love the idea of gathering—but don’t always have the time, capacity, or desire to do it all themselves.

You know the feeling: you want to invite people in, mark a moment, or finally host that holiday party—but the to-do list grows faster than your excitement. Suddenly, the celebration feels like another job.

If/When Events exists to take the weight off your shoulders. We focus on stress-free planning, warm hospitality, and details that feel like you, so you can be present with your people.

From first conversation to last candle blown out, we’re here to make sure your event is thoughtful, organized, and beautifully executed—without you having to manage every moving piece.

A few things we care about:

  • Events that feel welcoming and lived-in, not stiff or over-produced.
  • Clear communication so you always know what’s handled.
  • Honoring your budget, priorities, and comfort level.
  • Small touches that make guests feel seen and cared for.

However you like to celebrate—loud, cozy, or somewhere in between—we’ll help you bring that vision to life with intention and ease.
